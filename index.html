Why this club?

Do you have a special appreciation for notebooks that are filled with pages of perfectly crumpled paper?

Do you spend time just flipping through pages of past notebooks?

Then this is the club for you!!

How We Work

  • Those interested in joining must have a used notebook to bring to their first meeting
  • Each Sunday of the first of the month we will gather, exchange notebooks, and bask in each other's crumpled paper goodness
  • Members can bring used or new notebooks, or any kind of book with thin paper
